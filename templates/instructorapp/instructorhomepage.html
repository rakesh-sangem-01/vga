Overview

As an instructor, the Virtual Gardening Assistant helps you guide students, track their gardening progress, and provide expert advice. Monitor their tasks, offer personalized recommendations, and help them grow as gardeners.

Features

  • Track and manage student gardening tasks.
  • Monitor plant progress and suggest improvements.
  • Access and share expert gardening tips with students.
  • Provide personalized gardening recommendations based on student needs.
  • Contact support for any issues or queries related to gardening tasks.

Instructor Resources

  • Stay up to date with the latest gardening techniques and trends.
